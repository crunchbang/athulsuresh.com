I read the book after I had seen the movie, so I knew what was in  store. The book still managed to surprise me by going deep into some of  the dark corners that were left out from the movie. It adds a lot more  dimension to some of the characters and bit more backstory around some  of the events.

It's a tragic but endearing story; one that keeps you turning the pages.
