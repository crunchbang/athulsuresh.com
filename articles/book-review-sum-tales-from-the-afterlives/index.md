A wonderfully refreshing collection of short stories which offers a varying array of explanations as to what happens in the afterlife.

What I loved was how the author was able to give a meaningful commentary on human life through these disjointed stories. Each story provides ample food for thought and manages to touch upon some aspect of the living through its description of death and thereafter.

A short book that's jam packed with insights which I'll recommend to everyone.
